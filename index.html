Why it matters

More than a submission form

IEEE UNIBEN reviews your work, connects you with the right people, and helps strong projects go further than a shelf and a grade.

01

Built for real output

Every submission is reviewed against engineering standards. No participation awards. Projects that demonstrate actual depth get visibility — events, publications, and technical showcases.

02

Selective by design

The pipeline is deliberately selective. We review for technical credibility, problem clarity, and potential. If you make it through, it means something — to you, to recruiters, to the field.

03

Final year? This is for you.

Your FYP deserves more than a grade. IEEE UNIBEN can support you with journal submissions, co-authored research papers, and technical documentation that gives your work a life beyond graduation.

How it works

Three steps. No ambiguity.

Submit, get reviewed, get visible. The process is deliberate and fast.

01

Submit Your Idea

Fill the pipeline form completely. Include problem statement, proposed solution, stage, links, and what you need. Incomplete entries are deprioritised.

02

Review & Shortlist

IEEE UNIBEN leadership reviews submissions against technical merit, feasibility, and impact potential. Shortlisted founders are contacted directly.

03

Showcase Top Projects

Selected work is featured at IEEE events, published in The Betelgeuse Beacon, and supported for journal submission and research dissemination — especially for final year projects.
